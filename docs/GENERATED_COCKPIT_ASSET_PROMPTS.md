## Shared Direction

Use these constraints for every image:

```text
transparent background
first-person downhill MTB cockpit view
visible handlebar
visible gloved hands
visible brake levers
visible front suspension fork
partial front tire visible
snow downhill racing style
blue white orange color palette
fictional small grill turbo device near handlebar center
mobile game sprite, readable at 390x844 portrait
no real logos
no real athlete
no full rider body
no rear wheel
no team jersey
no text baked into the asset
no logos
```
